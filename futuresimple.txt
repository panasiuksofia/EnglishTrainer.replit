Tense: Future Simple

Usage:
1. To talk about a decision made at the moment of speaking.
   Example: I will answer the phone.

2. To express a future fact or prediction.
   Example: The sun will rise tomorrow.

3. To offer or promise to do something in the future.
   Example: I will help you with your homework.

Form:
- Affirmative: Subject + will + base verb
  Example: She will go to the party.

- Negative: Subject + will not (won't) + base verb
  Example: I won't be able to attend the meeting.

- Interrogative: Will + subject + base verb?
  Example: Will you come with me?

Time Expressions:
- tomorrow, next week, in the future, soon, in a few days
